Review Grabber Bookmarklet

One-click tool to extract all Google Maps reviews from the browser. No extensions, no installs.

Setup (one time)

  1. Drag the button below to your browser's bookmarks bar. If you don't see the bookmarks bar, press Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+B (Mac) to show it.
Grab Reviews
Tip: On mobile, you can't drag bookmarklets. Instead, bookmark this page, then edit the bookmark and replace the URL with the bookmarklet code. Or just use the desktop method — it's easier.

How to use (each time)

  1. Go to the Local Public Eatery Leaside Google Maps page.
  2. Click on the Reviews section so the reviews panel is open.
  3. Click the "Grab Reviews" bookmark in your bookmarks bar.
  4. Wait while it auto-scrolls through all reviews (you'll see a progress counter).
  5. When it says "Done", all reviews are copied to your clipboard.
  6. Go to the Leaderboard → Process Reviews tab → paste → click Parse Reviews.

What it does

  • Auto-scrolls the reviews panel to load every review
  • Extracts: reviewer name, star rating, date, and review text
  • Skips owner/business replies automatically
  • Formats everything and copies to clipboard
  • Shows a live progress counter while scrolling

Troubleshooting

  • "Could not find reviews panel" — Make sure you've clicked into the Reviews section on the Google Maps page. The reviews list should be visible and scrollable.
  • Clipboard blocked — Some browsers block clipboard access. A text box will appear instead; select all and copy manually.
  • Missing reviews — If Google Maps has hundreds of reviews, the scroll may take 30-60 seconds. Let it finish.
