Review Grabber Bookmarklet

One-click tool to extract Google Maps reviews for a specific month. No extensions, no installs.

Setup (one time)

  1. Drag the button below to your browser's bookmarks bar. If you don't see the bookmarks bar, press Ctrl+Shift+B (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+B (Mac) to show it.
Grab Reviews
Tip: On mobile, you can't drag bookmarklets. Instead, bookmark this page, then edit the bookmark and replace the URL with the bookmarklet code. Or just use the desktop method — it's easier.

How to use (each time)

  1. Go to the Local Public Eatery Leaside Google Maps page.
  2. Click on the Reviews section so the reviews panel is open.
  3. Click the "Grab Reviews" bookmark in your bookmarks bar.
  4. Pick the month you want to grab (e.g., "March 2026").
  5. Wait while it auto-scrolls. It will stop automatically once it reaches reviews from a previous month.
  6. When it says "Done", reviews for that month only are copied to your clipboard.
  7. Go to the Leaderboard → Process Reviews tab → paste → click Parse Reviews.

What it does

  • Asks you which month you want to grab (last 6 months available)
  • Auto-scrolls the reviews panel — stops when it passes your chosen month
  • Only extracts reviews that fall within the selected month
  • Skips reviews from other months (tells you how many were skipped)
  • Skips owner/business replies automatically
  • Copies everything to clipboard, formatted and ready to paste

Troubleshooting

  • "Could not find reviews panel" — Make sure you've clicked into the Reviews section on the Google Maps page. The reviews list should be visible and scrollable.
  • Clipboard blocked — Some browsers block clipboard access. A text box will appear instead; select all and copy manually.
  • Date accuracy — Google Maps shows relative dates ("2 weeks ago") which the tool converts to approximate dates. A small number of reviews near month boundaries may be included or excluded. This is normal.
